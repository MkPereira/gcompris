Prerequisites

CcItemView requires GObject.

Known Derived Interfaces

CcItemView is required by CcView.

Known Implementations

CcItemView is implemented by CcCamera, CcText, CcShape, CcItem, CcViewCellRenderer, CcPixbuf, CcLine, CcRoundedRectangle, CcViewWidget, CcCircle and CcRectangle.
